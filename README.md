> ### __Arithmancy__ [ˈær ɪθˌmæn si]<br>
> From Ancient Greek ἀριθμός (*arithmós*, “number”) + *-mancy*.<br>
> noun, uncountable
> <br><br>
> Method of divination consisting of __assigning numerical values__ to __characters__, __words__ or __phrases__.
